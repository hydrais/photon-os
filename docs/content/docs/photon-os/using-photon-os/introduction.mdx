---
title: Get Started with Photon OS
description: Create your Photon account and start using Photon OS
---

## Use Photon OS with the Photon HUD

1. Get a copy of the Photon HUD from the [Second Life Marketplace](#).
2. Attach the Photon HUD to your Second Life avatar and wait for it to load.
3. Create a new Photon account, or sign in if you already have one.
4. **Important:** Go to the Settings app, and to the Account section to link your Second Life account.

## Use Photon OS in a browser

1. Click [here](https://os.photon.hydrais.com) to launch Photon OS, or visit https://os.photon.hydrais.com
2. Create a new Photon account, or sign in if you already have one.

<Callout title="Linking a Second Life account requires Photon HUD">
  Photon OS can be used on any browser, but in order to link your Second Life
  account to your Photon account, you'll need to use the Photon HUD at least
  once.
</Callout>
